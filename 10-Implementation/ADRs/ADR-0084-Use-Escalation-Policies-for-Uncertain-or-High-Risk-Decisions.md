# ADR-0084: Use Escalation Policies for Uncertain or High-Risk Decisions

Status: Accepted

North Vector will use escalation policies when decisions are uncertain, high-risk, irreversible, or outside the system's delegated authority.

Escalation may include asking the user, requesting more information, requiring human approval, delaying execution, or routing to a higher-review workflow.

Outcome: North Vector avoids overreaching when uncertainty or consequence level exceeds safe automation boundaries.
