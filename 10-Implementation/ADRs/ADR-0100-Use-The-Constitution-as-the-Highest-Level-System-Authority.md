# ADR-0100: Use The Constitution as the Highest-Level System Authority

Status: Accepted

The North Vector Constitution shall serve as the highest-level governing authority for the platform.

Architectures, workflows, policies, agents, automations, recommendations, and future governance systems should remain subordinate to constitutional principles.

When lower-level rules conflict with constitutional intent, the Constitution takes precedence.

Outcome: North Vector gains a stable governing foundation capable of guiding future growth, complexity, and evolution.
