# ADR-0089: Use Governance Metrics and Health Indicators for System Stewardship

Status: Accepted

North Vector will track governance metrics and health indicators for the platform and its operating model.

Examples may include stale goals, unresolved approvals, failed workflows, memory-review backlog, outdated assumptions, cost anomalies, and data-retention issues.

These indicators should help identify system drift before it becomes structural failure.

Outcome: North Vector gains a management layer for monitoring the health of the personal operating system itself.
