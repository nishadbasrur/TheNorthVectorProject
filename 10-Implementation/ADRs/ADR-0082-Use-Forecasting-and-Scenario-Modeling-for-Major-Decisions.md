# ADR-0082: Use Forecasting and Scenario Modeling for Major Decisions

Status: Accepted

North Vector will use forecasting and scenario modeling for major decisions where uncertainty, timing, or tradeoffs materially affect outcomes.

The system should support multiple plausible futures rather than pretending that a single prediction is certain.

Scenario modeling may include best case, base case, downside case, and failure-mode analysis.

Outcome: North Vector improves decision quality by making uncertainty explicit and preparing for multiple possible futures.
