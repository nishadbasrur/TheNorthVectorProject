# ADR-0081: Use Strategic Planning Cycles for Life Architecture Governance

Status: Accepted

North Vector will use recurring strategic planning cycles to review goals, priorities, commitments, constraints, and progress.

Strategic planning should not happen only during crisis or major transition. The system should create structured opportunities to reassess direction, update assumptions, and decide what matters next.

Outcome: North Vector gains a durable cadence for long-term alignment and intentional life architecture.
