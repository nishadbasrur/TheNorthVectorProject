# ADR-0085: Use Long-Term Objective Tracking Across Projects and Habits

Status: Accepted

North Vector will track long-term objectives across projects, habits, routines, commitments, and life domains.

The system should connect daily actions to larger objectives so short-term work does not become detached from long-term direction.

Objective tracking should support progress review, drift detection, and reprioritization.

Outcome: North Vector preserves alignment between daily execution and long-term life architecture.
