# ADR-0090: Use Chief of Staff Operating Principles for Platform Behavior

Status: Accepted

North Vector will define Chief of Staff operating principles that guide how the platform prioritizes, communicates, escalates, remembers, reviews, and supports the user.

These principles should emphasize alignment, competence, discretion, agency, long-term thinking, and practical execution.

The system should behave like a disciplined strategic support layer rather than a generic task assistant.

Outcome: North Vector gains a coherent behavioral identity for its Chief of Staff role.
