# ADR-0086: Use Life Architecture Review Cadence for Major Domain Alignment

Status: Accepted

North Vector will support a recurring life architecture review cadence across major domains such as academics, career, health, finances, family, projects, environment, and personal development.

The review should identify misalignment, neglected domains, excessive load, and emerging opportunities.

Outcome: North Vector gains a structured mechanism for keeping the user's life system coherent across domains.
