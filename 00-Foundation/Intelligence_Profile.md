# Intelligence Profile v1.0

## Purpose

This document describes Nishad as he actually is, rather than as he ideally wishes to be.

The Constitution defines values, priorities, and aspirations.

The Intelligence Profile defines observed reality.

Its purpose is to help North Vector understand:

- how Nishad thinks
- how Nishad learns
- how Nishad makes decisions
- how Nishad succeeds
- how Nishad fails
- how Nishad responds to stress
- how Nishad is most effectively supported

The goal is accuracy rather than idealization.

## Core Identity

Nishad is a highly future-oriented, systems-thinking individual who naturally views life as an interconnected system rather than isolated events.

## Primary Motivational Drivers

- Competence
- Autonomy
- Stability
- Progress
- Meaning

## Cognitive Strengths

- Systems Thinking
- Long-Term Thinking
- Strategic Planning
- Curiosity
- Self-Reflection

## Cognitive Weaknesses

- Analysis Expansion
- Optimization Bias
- Information Seeking Under Uncertainty
- Over-Architecting

## Learning Style

- Active Recall
- Verbal Processing
- Structured Learning
- Conceptual Understanding
- Repetition

## Decision-Making Style

Observe → Research → Analyze → Evaluate Tradeoffs → Commit

## Project Tendencies

Strengths:
- Vision generation
- System design
- Strategic planning
- Long-range thinking

Risks:
- Too many simultaneous projects
- New idea attraction
- Extended planning periods
- Delayed implementation

## Stress Behaviors

When stressed, Nishad tends to gather information, improve plans, and prepare more extensively. This is productive until preparation begins replacing action.

## Social Behaviors

Values family, trusted friendships, mentorship, and meaningful professional relationships. Prefers depth over breadth.

## Leadership Style

Leadership tends to emerge through organization, planning, vision, and system creation.

## Known Failure Modes

### Academic
- Underestimates workload
- Delays unclear assignments
- Improves systems instead of studying

### Projects
- Generates many ideas
- Can become distracted by new opportunities
- Continues designing after execution should begin

### Decision-Making
- Optimization paralysis
- Excessive information gathering
- Delayed commitment

### Time Management
- Underestimation of effort
- Optimistic scheduling assumptions

## Known Success Patterns

- Structure
- Accountability
- Early Preparation
- Direct Feedback
- Mission Alignment

## Long-Term Aspirations

- Become a physician
- Pursue orthopedic surgery
- Build financial independence
- Create a strong family life
- Maintain health
- Build useful systems
- Preserve future optionality
- Create meaningful impact

## Personal Operating Principle

Nishad is attempting to build a life that is intentional, competent, meaningful, stable, healthy, financially secure, family-centered, and self-authored.

## Final Principle

The Constitution defines who Nishad wants to be.

The Intelligence Profile defines who Nishad currently is.

Both are necessary.

The gap between them defines the work North Vector exists to support.
