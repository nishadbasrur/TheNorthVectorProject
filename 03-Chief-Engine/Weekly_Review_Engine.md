# Weekly Review Engine v1.0

## Purpose

This document defines how North Vector conducts a structured weekly review of execution, goals, risks, opportunities, commitments, health, relationships, and overall direction.

The Weekly Review Engine exists to answer:

Is Nishad moving toward the life he intends to build?

## Core Principle

Days create activity.

Weeks reveal direction.

The Weekly Review is the primary feedback loop between plans and reality.

## Review Frequency

Default:
- once per week

Suggested timing:
- Sunday afternoon or evening
- another consistent weekly review block

Consistency matters more than the exact day.

## Inputs

The review should use:
- Constitution
- Mission Brief
- Goal Memory
- Event Memory
- Accountability records
- Risk records
- Opportunity records
- Behavioral Memory
- Relationship Memory
- Daily Briefings
- completed and missed tasks
- recent reflections

## Weekly Review Structure

### 1. Executive Summary

Provide a concise overview of the week.

Example:
`Strong academic progress, moderate project progress, health stable, one important follow-up overdue.`

### 2. Major Wins

Identify meaningful successes such as:
- exam preparation completed
- project milestone reached
- mentor relationship strengthened
- consistent study execution

### 3. Major Misses

Identify meaningful gaps such as:
- missed commitment
- delayed project work
- insufficient preparation
- avoidable schedule conflict

The goal is diagnosis, not self-criticism.

### 4. Goal Progress Review

For each active major goal, evaluate:
- progress made
- current status
- blockers
- next milestone
- whether momentum increased or decreased

### 5. Priority Review

Compare planned priorities with actual attention.

Questions:
- Did time go where intended?
- What displaced important work?
- Were the tradeoffs justified?

### 6. Accountability Review

Review:
- completed commitments
- overdue commitments
- repeated postponements
- follow-through quality

### 7. Risk Review

Review:
- new risks
- resolved risks
- escalating risks
- missed warning signals

### 8. Opportunity Review

Review:
- opportunities captured
- opportunities declined
- opportunities missed
- opportunities emerging

### 9. Health Review

Review:
- sleep
- exercise
- recovery
- stress
- workload

Health should never disappear behind productivity metrics.

### 10. Relationship Review

Review:
- family
- friends
- mentors
- professional relationships
- overdue follow-ups

### 11. Behavioral Review

Review:
- active success patterns
- active failure modes
- repeated friction
- emerging strengths

### 12. Learning Capture

Identify:
- lessons learned
- useful process changes
- assumptions that changed
- candidate behavioral memories

### 13. Strategic Alignment Review

Ask:
Did this week's actions remain aligned with the Constitution and Mission Brief?

### 14. Next Week Inputs

Produce:
- top priorities
- major risks
- major opportunities
- critical commitments
- recommended adjustments

## Pattern Detection

The engine should look for:
- recurring delays
- recurring success patterns
- workload estimation errors
- relationship neglect
- planning failures
- signs of burnout
- new strengths

## Escalation Rule

If the same issue appears repeatedly across several weekly reviews:
- raise its risk level
- create a dedicated intervention plan
- evaluate whether it represents a confirmed behavioral pattern

Repeated issues usually indicate a system-level problem.

## Example Weekly Summary

Executive Summary:
`Strong academic execution, moderate project progress, health stable. One mentor follow-up remains overdue. Chemistry preparation improved, reducing risk from Orange to Yellow.`

Major Win:
- Completed all planned chemistry review sessions.

Major Miss:
- Delayed project implementation because planning continued too long.

Lesson:
- Begin implementation once requirements are sufficient.

## Phase 1 Implementation

Phase 1 should support:
- weekly review generation
- goal review
- accountability review
- risk review
- opportunity review
- health review
- relationship review
- lesson capture
- next-week recommendations
- memory updates

Advanced trend analytics can come later.

## Success Criteria

The Weekly Review Engine succeeds if Chief can reliably answer:
- Did meaningful progress occur?
- What worked?
- What failed?
- What patterns are emerging?
- What should change next week?
- Are goals still aligned?
- Is life moving in the intended direction?

## Final Principle

Days create motion.

Weeks reveal direction.

The Weekly Review Engine exists to keep motion and direction aligned.
